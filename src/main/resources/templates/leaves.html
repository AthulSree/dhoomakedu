Leave Portal Guide

  • 1. Select Employee Choose the correct personnel from the dropdown list.
  • 2. Absent Dates Enter just the days (e.g. 2, 13) if 2nd and 13th was absent. Comma-separated values only. FYI: Focus on this field and click Enter/Tab/Space to see what Shammy says!
  • 3. Half-Day Leaves (Day/2) Use this to mention half-day leaves. Add half-day counts only for those who have the work order from AKAL. Should be entered only if strictly necessary (Nannaayitt alochichitt mathram).
  • 4. Used Combo Leaves Mention all the leaves, including combo leaves, as comma-separated values. The combo will be applied to it directly.
  • 5. Upload Attendance (Optional) Uploading the PDF is optional. If uploaded, the system will automatically print available, used, and pending combo leaves on the document.
